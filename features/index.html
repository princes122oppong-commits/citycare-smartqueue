Everything You Need for Seamless Queue Management

Online Appointment Booking

Patients can book, reschedule, or cancel appointments anytime from any device. Our intelligent scheduling system ensures optimal time slots and reduces no-shows with automated reminders.

  • 24/7 online booking from any device
  • Automated SMS and email reminders
  • Smart slot optimization to reduce gaps
  • Easy rescheduling and cancellation

Real-time Mobile Queueing

Track your position in the queue from your smartphone. Receive live updates when your turn is approaching, so you can wait comfortably without sitting in a crowded waiting room.

  • Live queue position tracking
  • Push notifications for status changes
  • Estimated wait time display
  • Virtual check-in from anywhere

SMS & Email Notifications

Keep patients informed every step of the way with automated notifications. From appointment confirmations to queue updates, our system ensures clear and timely communication.

  • Queue status alerts and updates
  • Delay notifications with new ETAs
  • Post-visit feedback requests

Analytics & Reporting

Gain deep insights into clinic performance, patient flow, and staff efficiency. Data-driven reports help administrators make informed decisions to continuously improve service delivery.

  • Real-time dashboard with key metrics
  • Patient flow and wait time analytics
  • Staff performance reports
  • Exportable data for further analysis

Secure Role-based Access

Our platform provides tailored experiences for patients, receptionists, department staff, and administrators. Each role has appropriate access levels to ensure data security and privacy.

  • Admin dashboard with full oversight
